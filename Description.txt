A sequence of data about applicants nameList of type Entrant and a sequence of integers
yearList representing years are given. Each element of the nameList sequence includes
the fields School number, Year of entering, Last name. Get data (a list of YearSchoolStat
values) about the number of different schools that applicants graduated from, for each
year from the yearList. YearSchoolStat includes Year of entering, Number of Schools
fields. If in the given year of admission there are no applicants from the listed schools,
indicate zero in the field Number of schools. The YearSchoolStat list must be sorted in
ascending order by the number of schools, and for matching values, in ascending order
by the year number.
